Pricing FAQ

Can I switch between subscription plans?

Yes, you can switch between subscription plans at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the new rate will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

How to cancel or pause a subscription

You can cancel or pause your subscription at any time from your account settings. If you cancel, you'll still have access to your plan's features until the end of your current billing period.

How many users can I add in each plan

Both Basic and Pro plans allow unlimited team members. The main difference is in the number of projects and features available to those team members.

What happens to my accounts when my trial period ends?

When your trial period ends, your account will automatically switch to the free plan with limited features. To maintain access to premium features, you'll need to upgrade to a paid plan before the trial ends.
